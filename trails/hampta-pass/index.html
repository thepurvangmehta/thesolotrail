Hampta Pass

🇮🇳 India 4,270m 5–6 days Moderate+

Two completely different worlds
separated by one pass.

The Hampta Pass crossing is one of India's most dramatic landscape transitions. Start in the lush, forested Kullu valley. Cross the 4,270m pass. Emerge into the stark, lunar terrain of Lahaul — all within a single day's walk. It's a trek that makes you understand why mountain passes defined ancient trade routes.
